Geomap

 

Geografic map over time showing 9 sensors paramaters,

 

In this map we see the wetlands in Kalmar, close to Kalmar Öland Airport. See the map in Google Maps ><

On the map you can see 9 circles that represent 9 sensors that were installed in 9 differents points of the wetlands sorrounding the airport in order to control water depth, conductivity of the water

To see the values you can move the cursos over the circle. You also can stop the animation pressing PAUSE button.

 

 

Detail of the istance sensor LDDS75

 

 

Conductivity (µSiemens/cm"), Water depth (cm) and Temperature (°C) .

 

 

Diagram over time showing 4 variables

This diagram show the relationship between Conductivity, Waterdepth and Temperature over time.

When pressing PLAY you can see how the values change:

  • Conductivity is represented on the x-axis (horizontal axis).
  • Water depth is represented on the y-axis (vertical axis).
  • Temperature is related to the size and color of the circle.
  • NOTE: to see the values you can move the cursos over the circle. You also can stop the animation pressing PAUSE button.
